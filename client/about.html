  >>> About Spellblade:

  >>> Spellblade is a text-based MMORPG where you can venture across a mystical world with an adventurer of your very own!

  >>> You can customize your character, fight monsters, explore, go on quests, make friends, and more!

  >>> Spellblade is currently early in development, but new features will be made available as they are developed! Checkout the "source" button above if you want to help with the game's development.

  >>> Thanks for checking it out, and happy adventuring! -Mohsin
